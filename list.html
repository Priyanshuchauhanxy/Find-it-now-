Lost items:

Clicking the "Lost Something" button initiates a streamlined process
for users to input details about their lost belongings.Fields may include
item description, date and location of loss, and any unique identifiers.

Found items:

The "Found Something" button allows users to register items they've found,
contributing to a centralized database.Similar to lost item registration, it
prompts users to provide relevant details, creating a comprehensive record.

List of found items:

The software maintains a dynamic list of found items, facilitating easy browsing
for both users who lost items and those who found them.Each entry includes key
information about the item, making it accessible and efficient.
